This strategy is based on the WMA indicator. It opens a position everytime that the close > WMA and sell it everytime close < WMA. the lenght of periodsUsed in this strategy was 9 using the data of BTC-USD in 1m dataframe. The backtest info was from yfinances and was applied in the interval of 2023-03-27 from 2023-04-02. The total profit was: 106.99 with a maximun drawdown of -5.21.
